AgentDrop — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

AgentDrop is a personal automation tool that creates short-form vertical videos and publishes them to the operator's own social media accounts (such as TikTok and YouTube). This policy explains what data it handles.

Who this applies to

AgentDrop is operated by a single individual for their own accounts. It is not a multi-tenant service and does not create accounts for other people.

Information we access

  • Authorization tokens. When you connect a TikTok or YouTube account, the platform provides an OAuth access/refresh token that lets AgentDrop post videos and read analytics on that account. Tokens are stored privately by the operator and are never shared.
  • Performance metrics. AgentDrop reads public metrics for its own posts and account — views, likes, comments, follower/subscriber counts — via the platforms' official APIs, and stores them to track performance over time.

How information is used

Data is used solely to (a) publish AgentDrop's own videos to the connected accounts and (b) report performance back to the operator. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Data remains in the operator's own private database. The only external services contacted are the platforms whose APIs are used to post and read stats (e.g. TikTok, YouTube/Google).

Retention & revocation

Stored metrics and tokens are kept only as long as needed to operate AgentDrop. You can revoke AgentDrop's access at any time from your TikTok or Google account's connected-apps settings, which immediately invalidates its tokens.
