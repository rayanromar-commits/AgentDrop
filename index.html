StoryDropper

Automatically creates short vertical storytelling videos and posts them to your own TikTok and YouTube accounts.

What it does

  • Turns short written stories into narrated, captioned 9:16 videos.
  • Publishes them to the creator's own connected social accounts on a schedule.
  • Reports back performance (views, likes, followers) so the creator can see what's working.

How it uses TikTok

  • Login Kit — the creator authorizes their own account.
  • Content Posting API — uploads finished videos to the creator's TikTok inbox to review and post.
  • Profile & video stats — reads the creator's own follower/view/like counts to report growth.
