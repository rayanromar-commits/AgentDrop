TIFU by microwaving my coworker's coffee for ten minutes
subreddit: tifu

This happened this morning and I'm still cringing. I work in a small office and we share one tiny kitchen. My coworker Dave always leaves his mug of coffee on the counter and reheats it throughout the day, which I find a little gross but whatever.

This morning I grabbed what I thought was MY mug (we have the same plain white ones) and popped it in the microwave. I meant to hit one minute but I was half asleep and typed in ten. Then I walked off to a meeting and completely forgot about it.

Twenty minutes later the whole office smells like burnt tar. The coffee had boiled down to a black crust and the mug actually cracked. Turns out it was Dave's mug, a gift from his daughter. He wasn't even mad, just quietly sad, which made it a thousand times worse. I'm buying him a new one today.
