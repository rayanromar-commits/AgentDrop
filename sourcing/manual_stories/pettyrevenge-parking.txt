Petty revenge on the neighbor who kept stealing my parking spot
subreddit: pettyrevenge

I live in a building where we each pay extra for an assigned parking spot. Mine is number 14, closest to the door, which I pay a premium for. For three weeks straight, a guy from the fourth floor kept parking his shiny new truck in my spot because, in his words, "you weren't using it at the time."

I left polite notes. He laughed them off. I told the building manager, who "sent an email." Nothing changed. So I got petty.

I found out the visitor spots had a strict two-hour limit, enforced by a towing company the building was contracted with. One morning I parked my own car in a visitor spot on purpose, then called the manager to "report a suspicious long-term car" in visitor parking. The tow company came that afternoon.

The truck? Still illegally in my spot, right next to the visitor zone. The driver hooked the wrong vehicle by plate. His brand-new truck got towed across town, and he paid two hundred dollars to get it back. He parks on the street now.
