Strange man at my house at 3:30 AM
subreddit: creepyencounters
I'm female and live alone. I live at the end of a townhouse complex tucked away at the end of the driveway, and instead of a garage I have a large open carport.

My NBN box (for internet in Australia, where you connect the ethernet cable from your router) is outside on the brick wall in the car port. On the other side of that wall is my bedroom, more importantly, my bed.

A few nights ago at 3:30 AM I woke up to a weird sound on the other side of the wall. I went and looked and saw a strange man walking down the driveway. When I went into the carport, the light was on, the power point was turned off and the ethernet cable ripped out and the port was broken. He had taken the cover off the NBN box and that's what woke me up- when he was putting it back on.

Pretty sure he was looking for a spare key so he could get into my house because this is a classic spare key hidey hole, and quickly bailed when he realised there wasn't one. Annoyingly, he also broke the ethernet port, leaving me with no internet for days. I've never been so relieved that I don't have a spare key.
